History

It all started with an accident.
Shitman was in the greatest fight of his life when
he saw a sword coming right for his face!
What did he do? That crazy bastard male who
was raised by both of his real parents, ducked
the attack and sliced the enemy’s legs off.
It was a glorious day. And after that day,
Shitman decided to start this company.
